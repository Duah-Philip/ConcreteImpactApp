# ConcreteImpactApp 1.0.0

## Initial Release (2025-03-16)

### New Features
* Interactive Shiny dashboard for cradle-to-gate LCA of U.S. ready-mix concrete
* **Fly Ash (FA) module**: 16 scenarios across three treatment technologies
  (Thermal, Electrostatic, Chemical), three ash sources (Fresh Ash, Landfill,
  Impoundment), and two LOI/dose levels; with and without avoided-impact credit
* **Slag (GGBFS) module**: four supply-chain origins (USA, Brazil, China, Japan)
  at three replacement levels (30%, 40%, 50%)
* **Mix Design viewer**: material input cards and downloadable tables for all
  six compressive strength classes (17.2–55.2 MPa)
* **LCIA Results (Absolute)**: interactive 10-panel subplot via plotly, one
  panel per TRACI 2.2 impact category, on independent y-axes
* **Percent Change vs Baseline**: ggplot2 facet plot across all compressive
  strengths and replacement levels, styled after the reference publication
* **Comparison tab**: side-by-side scatter comparison of all FA and slag
  scenarios for any selected impact category and compressive strength
